 IDEA:
 You and your copilot have been entrusted with the important task of finding habitable parallel worlds.

 Earth high command gave you notice that the pilot that finds more habitable worlds will be promoted.
 Cognisant of the incentive structure placed before you, you notice that in addition to finding as
 many habitable worlds as possible, you need to prevent your copilot from finding as many as possible.

 You and your copilot made a bet who will find more habitable worlds. You notice, that to win this bet
 you not only need to find as many habitable worlds yourself, but also need to prevent your copilot from
 finding as many habitable worlds as possible.
  => So you win each mission if either:
      - you can land on a habitable world.
      - or your copilot fails to land on a habitable world on their shift.
